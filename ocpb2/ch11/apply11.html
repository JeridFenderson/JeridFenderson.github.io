How to Build Good Credit

I wrote an entire book on finances called The Best Dollar You'll Ever Spend.Go check it out on Amazon! Search my name, Jerid Fenderson, and both my books pop up.

To sum it up, building credit is a long game, it takes time. As Warren Buffet once said, you can't have a baby in a month by getting 9 women pregnant. Start with what you can, and it'll grow from there, so long as you pay your debt on time.

You don't have to do too many things right, so long as you don't do too many things wrong.

  • 1. Get a credit card
  • 2. Have less than 10% of the limit get posted to the statement every month by paying it down before the statement closing date
  • 3. Ask for a credit limit increase
  • 4. Repeat

Also consider utilizing loans to help build your credit. But like I said, I wrote a whole book on the topic and I advise you to check it out.
